Our contact details

Elevador is located in the Poblenou district of Barcelona.

The address is Carrer de Llull 267 (bajos 4), 08005 Barcelona, telephone 611 017 372. You can reach us by metro L4 (yellow line), stop Poblenou. There are two Bicing stations closeby.


Opening hours

Elevador does not have strict opening hours: these depend on the activities that are organised. Workshops and events may take place in evenings or weekends. A separate part of Elevador is used as office space and usually there will be someone present between 10.00 and 18.00 hrs.

You are welcome to visit us, just let us know and we will find an opportunity when the space is not used for activites.
